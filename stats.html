Statistics

Cloud9 has won over 50 tournaments as an orginzation, with major wins in games such as CS:GO, League of Legends, Rocket League, Valorant, and Smash Bros. Melee. In 2020 alone they took 13 first place victories, and have taken 8 second, third and fourth places. Some of their most notable wins have been being the first North American team to win a Major in CS:GO as well as placing in the top 8 of the League of Legends 2021 World Championship.
